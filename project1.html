Hellmann's Campaign

Client - Hellmann's, the world's leading mayonnaise brand since 1913, renowned for its rich and creamy flavor that elevates everyday dishes.

Problem - Despite its market dominance, Hellmann's faces increased competition from brands like Heinz and supermarket own labels. Hellmann’s wants to raise the bar and set itself apart from the copy-cats and make its superiority UNMISSABLE once again

Insight - Taking an indirect approach by Showing the copied/cheap knockoffs of famous brands to encourage them to get the original, no. 1 product.
